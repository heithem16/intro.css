PhD in Telecommunications at the National Engineering School of Tunis-El Manar University (Defended on: 13/03/2021 with honorable mention)
I started my Doctoral Study in the Department of computer engineering in 2015 September. I worked on Performance of different frequency reconfigurable antenna’s systems for different wireless applications in my thesis. My PhD supervisor was Prof. Taoufik AGUILI. My PhD thesis title is “Electromagnetic modeling of reconfigurable antennas for wireless communications”.

My thesis deal with the development of an electromagnetic model based on integro-differential equations that aim to predict the response of a reconfigurable antenna radiated in free space. I proposed an overall formulation based on MoM-GEC method for modelling antennas and antenna arrays. Obtained results show that the proposed antennas structures allow to generate several modes corresponding to different resonance frequencies. The resulting frequencies are suitable for different wireless applications such as Radar, Satellite, RFID, WiMAX, WLAN and 5G applications.
